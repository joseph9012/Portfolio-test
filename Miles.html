The Problem
The process of hiring a car currently requires a human to check each qoute.
The Aim
Create a mobile app that allows the user to rent high end vehicles without the need for human approval on Miles and More's end.
Miles and More need a mobile app for rental of home-delivered luxury cars from Miles & More in London. They currently have a website but all requests and quotes must go via a human for approval. This not only makes a long wait time for the customer as they have to wait for their request to be checked manually but also time consuming for the team at Miles and More.
The aim of this project is to design a high end mobile app allowing cumsters to easily and quickly rent high end cars. Freeing up the time fo the Miles and More team so they can spend their time more benifically on other tasks and improving the service they offer.
